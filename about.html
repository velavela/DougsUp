Our Story

We are an Austin based small business specializing in upholstery repairs and restorations. We are woman owned and operated and love working with the service sector- our upholstery can be seen in bars and tattoo parlors throughout Austin, and we may have given a second life to your hairdresser's swivel chair or massage therapist's table!

We also have a passion for upcycling and an inventory of one of a kind pieces that we have deconstructed and overhauled with our own personal designs. These unique pieces combine our creativity with our goal of having less perfectly usable furniture end up in landfills.

Our roots are in Abilene, our new family in Austin, and our artist and craft community is ever-expanding. Please don't hesitate to reach out with inquiries, collaboration ideas, or just to say hello!
